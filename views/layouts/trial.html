My Services

PMP

I can help you achieve your project goal by taking on the responsibility of the planning, procurement, and executing of a well-developed plan. I will ensure that all impacted users understand the defined scope, defined start and defined finish. I will ensure that risk are managed in a way that reduces the risk of overall failure, maximizes benefits, and minimizes costs.

CIO

As CIO, I will be your business leader. I will help executives make decisions regarding the purchasing of IT equipment from suppliers or the creation of new systems. I will recruit, lead and direct the IT workforce so you have the best employees to complete the projects on-time and on budget. Finally, I will map out both the strategic plan and IT policies.

Full Stack Web Developer

As part of your team, I will create web user friendly websites that make the user experience very easy. I can provide server side programing as well.
