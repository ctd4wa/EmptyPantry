EmptyPantry

EmptyPantry is a full-stack web application that allows you to take full advantage of what groceries you have in your house

Simply log in or register for the application and click on the search tab to find put in your ingredients into the form.

Upon your request EmptyPantry will fetch you a curated list of recipes you can create based on the ingredients you entered
